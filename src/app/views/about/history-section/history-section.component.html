About Afriglobal

Brief History

Afriglobal Group began operations in 1988 by a visionary man Mr. B. B. Garg. Our business was majorly raw chemical distribution. We have since grown into a diversified entity with about five dynamic and fast growing competent organizations now referred to as Afriglobal Group of Companies. Each organization has developed into the market leader or one of the leading players in its own field.

Currently, beyond Nigeria, we have operations in Cameroun, Ghana, Djibouti, Ethiopia, Tanzania, and Egypt. We also have offices India, China and South Korea. However, its international outlook notwithstanding, Afriglobal group has remained true to its African Origin.
