Socially Responsible Company

At Afriglobal Logistics, our CSR activities are guided by our vision which is to “inspire Africa”. Through our CSR activities, we work to advance our corporate citizenship, build relationships of mutual trust with stake holders and aim for sustainable growth.

Through our foundation - Nagode Foundation, we is actively involved in several socio-economic projects that positively affect the lives of African people. We believe in giving back to the communities in whom we have become who are thereby staying true to our values. Every year we commit some percentages of our profit solely to execute worthy projects that will leave a lasting impact in the lives of people.
