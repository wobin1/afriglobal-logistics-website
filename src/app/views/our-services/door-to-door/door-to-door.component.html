Afriglobal Logistics core business is focused on delivery any type of material on a door-to-door basis to its clients.

The service starts from expediting of the purchase orders placed abroad by the client and ends when the goods is delivered to its warehouse/premises anywhere into the Nigerian territory.

The same concept/system is applied also to export cargo from any origin point in Nigeria to any country of destination. Partnership and alliances in place with major global freight forwarders is the key factor of the success of such concept.
